About Me

I love technology! Some people are content to be end-users. I enjoy dissecting a product’s many parts from concept to design to market appeal. When I fix a bug or discover a new way to solve an issue, I'm elated. In the world of software development, all problems are solvable and I have every tool that I need at my fingertips. Programming allows my team to create websites and applications that are as unique and interesting as our collective community.

When I'm not devouring new programming resources, I'm enjoying some old school tabletop gaming or reading a book. Speculative Fiction and Nordic Noir are my favorite genres.
